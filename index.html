Blockchain for Global Good

International Workshop on Blockchain
Organized by VJTI Mumbai


Blockchain being a disruptive technology provides immense opportunities to the government and industry, to design solutions for real-life problems. Blockchains are much more beyond crypto-currencies and offer the promise of a trustworthy way to record shared data. The Important Idea that makes blockchains useful is to distribute the maintenance of the blockchain to many (possibly distrusting) parties, thereby achieving “distributed consensus” in real time.
This international workshop facilitates interactions for better understanding of technological developments in Blockchain through keynote technical talks, technology showcase demos. Eminent speakers of international repute will be presenting technology foundations and interesting use cases
